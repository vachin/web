About

Vachin came from the Sanskrit word वचन (vácana) which has two meanings like

  1. promise, word, oath, vow
  2. word, utterance, speech

So by considering the second meaning Vachin is the curated store of texts, quotes, messages which we generally use to express ourselves.

The inspiration behind Vachin is that most of us can't express our feelings in words. And in layman terms only a few can express their feelings with words and the rest have better feelings but they can't express them.

Vachin aggregate best sentences from people and history and sort them with relevancy. So that who in need of those messages or quotes can use them anywhere they are.

We will maintain this crowd sourced texts always available for all kinds of users across the world and coming soon in more languages.

We need to thank Unsplash It for their beautiful placeholder images which we are using as background for our texts. And soon we are planning to attribute each contributor of those images.

We provide free access to our APIs which is in progress and it will be available soon through https://dev.vach.in which is for developers.
